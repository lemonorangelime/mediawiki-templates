# Template:Wikitable

generates an information table

## Usage example: 

{{{wikitable}}
! Column 1
! Column 2
|-
| 1 || 2
|-
| 3 || 4
|}


### Creates this table:

---------------------
| Column1 | Column2 |
---------------------
| 1       | 2       |
---------------------
| 3       | 4       |
---------------------
